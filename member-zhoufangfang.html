| Introduction

  • PhD stage: studied the effects of altitude on the reproductive biological characteristics of perennial legume forage. The changes of cell morphology during pollen fertilization and embryonic development were investigated by histomorphology, and the mechanism of regulating factors related to embryo abortion was investigated by molecular biology. Physiological changes of nutrients, sugars and hormones during development were studied by physiological and biochemical indexes. Combined with environmental factors, the main factors affecting the reproduction of perennial vetch were determined through principal component and correlation analysis.
  • Master stage: mainly studied on " tissue culture rooting mechanism of peony " and participated in the research of "distant cross incompatibility of Peony" and "separation, purification and substance identification of peony rooting cofactors".
